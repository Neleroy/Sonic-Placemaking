# Sonic-Placemaking
A repository for DIY experimental instruments, created for the [Centre for Art Tapes' 2025-26 Media Art Scholarship](https://www.cfat.ca/mas-2026).

![Screech Promo Image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a10a3db-dea0-4740-82b5-70168d654cb9)

The 38th Annual Media Arts Scholarship Exhibition, entitled Here and Now, was held at the Centre for Art Tapes in Halifax, Nova Scotia from March 20th to April 3rd, 2026. My contribution was a series of six experimental electronic instruments (with all but one having an additional companion module), hung from the ceiling by their audio cables. All electronics, save for the Digi-Quack, are hand-assembled and analogue, and all enclosures are 3D printed with the aid of Hunyuan3D (https://huggingface.co/spaces/tencent/Hunyuan3D-2.1) and Mable Monroe.
